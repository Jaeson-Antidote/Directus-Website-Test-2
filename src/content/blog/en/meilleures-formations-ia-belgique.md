## Academic AI training programmes

These university programmes are aimed at students and graduates who want a thorough theoretical and practical understanding of AI, often research-oriented.

### KU Leuven — Master of artificial intelligence

This master's programme trains a broad range of students across all areas of knowledge-based technology, with a notable specialisation in intelligent robotics.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 1-2 years
- **Price:** 1,000 - 2,000 €
- **Specialisations:** General AI, robotics

</div>

### UCLouvain — Master in computer science (strong AI component)

Focused on research and technological innovation, this master's offers solid AI training, preparing students for tomorrow's technical challenges.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 1-2 years
- **Price:** 1,000 - 2,000 €
- **Specialisations:** Data science, machine learning

</div>

### ULB — Master in artificial intelligence

Renowned for its interdisciplinary approach, this programme combines robotics, humanities, and industrial applications.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 2 years
- **Price:** 1,000 - 2,000 €
- **Specialisations:** AI, machine learning, NLP

</div>

### ULiège — Master in machine learning and big data

If your interest lies in large-scale data analysis and machine learning, this master's is the natural choice: ULiège excels in these fields.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 1-2 years
- **Price:** 1,000 - 2,000 €
- **Specialisations:** Big data, machine learning

</div>

### Hasselt University — Master in applied artificial intelligence

This programme focuses on the practical application of AI in various industrial and social contexts.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 1-2 years
- **Price:** 1,000 - 2,000 €
- **Specialisations:** Applied AI

</div>

## Bootcamps and career transition programmes

Bootcamps are an intensive alternative to university courses. Ideal for quickly acquiring practical AI skills and making a career transition.

### Le Wagon — Data science bootcamp

This intensive bootcamp develops the skills needed for a career in data science, with a focus on Python, SQL, and machine learning. Includes portfolio creation.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 9 weeks
- **Price:** 6,000 - 8,000 €
- **Specialisations:** Data science, Python

</div>

### Cercle IA — Executive training in generative AI

Specifically designed for advisory professionals (lawyers, senior consultants, accounting professionals), this programme includes masterclasses and individual sessions in small groups.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 6 masterclasses + 2 individual sessions
- **Price:** On request
- **Specialisations:** Generative AI for advisory roles

</div>

## Professional training for executives

These programmes are designed for active managers and executives who want to integrate AI into their business strategy without interrupting their work.

### IHECS Academy — AI for your content production

An intensive 2-day training to master generative AI tools (Midjourney, DALL-E, Runway, Heygen) applied to content creation.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 2 days (14 hours)
- **Price:** 645 €
- **Specialisations:** AI-powered visual, video, and audio content production

</div>

### ICHEC Formation Continue — AI strategy & deployment certificate

These certificates aim to help participants understand how AI can be integrated into business strategies, up to the level of Chief AI Officer.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 10-15 days
- **Price:** 2,500 - 4,500 €
- **Specialisations:** AI strategy, business

</div>

### HEC Liège — Specialisation certificate in artificial intelligence

This programme combines mandatory and optional modules to understand the challenges of AI and its impact within your organisation.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** Minimum 42 hours (6+ days)
- **Price:** 2,500 - 4,500 €
- **Specialisations:** AI strategy

</div>

## Short and introductory training

For a first introduction or awareness-raising, these programmes are suitable for all levels.

### Google Digital Workshops — Free online training

Accessible to all levels, from beginners to experienced developers, these programmes provide an introduction to the basics of AI.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 25 hours
- **Price:** Free
- **Specialisations:** Core concepts

</div>

### Quality Training — Short courses

Specialising in improving office and IT skills, Quality Training offers express sessions on ChatGPT and the integration of AI into Microsoft 365 tools.

<div class="formation-entry">

- **Duration:** 3 hours to a few days
- **Price:** 300 - 800 €
- **Specialisations:** ChatGPT, Copilot, AI basics

</div>
