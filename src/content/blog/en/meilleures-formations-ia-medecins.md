The integration of AI into the medical sector represents a major strategic challenge for doctors and healthcare professionals. From diagnostic imaging to clinical decision support systems, clinical and administrative applications are multiplying.

To practise effectively in this changing environment, doctors, nurses, hospital managers, and administrators need to develop solid AI competencies.

This article lists the most relevant and recognised training programmes, structured by learning format.

*Note: prices and durations are given as indications and may change depending on institutions and academic years. We recommend checking directly on official websites at the time of enrolment.*

---

## Academic training and university certificates

### Inter-university certificate in artificial intelligence in medicine and digital health (ULB/UMons, Belgium)

The reference programme in Belgium. A 10 ECTS credit certificate covering assisted diagnosis, medical imaging, telehealth, and ethical and legal issues.

- **Format:** hybrid (Fridays 5:30–9 pm and Saturdays 9 am–1 pm)
- **Duration:** October 2025 to June 2026 (~120 hours)
- **Cost:** 1,200 € (full), 600 € (reduced), 350 € (job seekers)
- **Audience:** doctors, nurses, hospital administrators, no AI prerequisites
- **Language:** French

### University diploma in applied artificial intelligence in healthcare (Université Paris Cité, France)

A university diploma structured in 15 modules. Addresses the systemic, economic, regulatory, and ethical challenges of AI in a medical context.

- **Format:** in-person + synchronous distance learning (Thursdays)
- **Duration:** 90 hours + 15 hours personal work | January to May 2026
- **Cost:** 2,000 €
- **Audience:** doctors, healthcare professionals, scientific professionals
- **Language:** French

### Artificial intelligence in healthcare (Université Lyon 1, France)

A multidisciplinary university diploma combining scientific, ethical, and humanistic approaches. A recognisable qualification in the job market.

- **Format:** 5 two-day seminars
- **Duration:** 90 hours
- **Cost:** 804 € (initial training), 2,000 € (continuing education)
- **Audience:** doctors, healthcare professionals, engineers
- **Language:** French

## Online training and MOOCs

### AI+Health: techniques, applications, ethics (Université Laval, Canada)

An academically rigorous introductory programme. 5 modules covering AI basics, health data, ethics, and health innovations.

- **Format:** self-paced e-learning
- **Duration:** ~10 hours
- **Cost:** 90 €
- **Audience:** all levels
- **Key strength:** emphasis on medical ethics
- **Language:** French

### AI training for doctors and healthcare professionals (Cercle IA, Belgium)

A practical programme focused on the concrete use of AI in everyday medical practice. A 3-session programme covering the use of AI for report writing, patient file analysis, literature review, and patient communication.

- **Format:** online
- **Duration:** ~6 hours
- **Cost:** 750 € excl. VAT
- **Audience:** GPs, specialists, interns, nurses
- **Key strength:** hands-on approach with real medical cases, small group (max 8 participants)
- **Language:** French

### AI in healthcare specialization (Stanford University, Coursera)

A 5-course specialisation exploring the safe and ethical integration of AI in clinical practice. Current and future applications, improving patient safety.

- **Format:** flexible online
- **Cost:** 49 €/month
- **Audience:** clinical and administrative teams
- **Key strength:** Stanford expertise, ethics and safety focus
- **Language:** English

### Executive Master AI & E-Health + short courses (INeS, France)

A flexible offering combining a full master's programme (70 hours, 2,250 €) and short thematic MOOCs (2-10 hours, free to 350 €). Applied content based on concrete cases.

- **Format:** modular e-learning
- **Audience:** time-constrained doctors, administrators
- **Key strength:** modularity and operational applicability
- **Language:** French

## Specialised training and practical workshops

### MIT Sloan artificial intelligence in healthcare (MIT Management Education)

A 6-week programme for sector executives. Understanding the strategic potential of AI in healthcare organisations: technologies, applications, limitations, and opportunities.

- **Format:** online
- **Duration:** 6 weeks
- **Cost:** 2,650 €
- **Audience:** managers, executives, strategists
- **Key strength:** MIT recognition, managerial approach
- **Language:** English

### AI in healthcare (Harvard Medical School Executive Education)

An 8-week programme for leaders and decision-makers. Design, evaluate, and implement AI solutions. Covers ethics, regulation, system validation, and strategic roadmapping.

- **Format:** online with live sessions
- **Duration:** 8 weeks
- **Cost:** 3,050 €
- **Audience:** healthcare sector leaders and strategists
- **Key strength:** strategic and ethical approach, Harvard recognition
- **Language:** English

### Generative AI and prompt engineering in healthcare (NobleProg, Belgium/Europe)

A practitioner-focused training on applied generative AI: diagnostic assistance, clinical record writing, patient communication. GDPR compliance and confidentiality built in.

- **Format:** 1-2 intensive days | in-person/remote/in-company
- **Cost:** 2,550 € (online) / 3,100 € (in-person in Brussels)
- **Audience:** doctors, hospital managers, innovation teams
- **Key strength:** immediately applicable in practice
- **Language:** French

### AI in healthcare (Johns Hopkins University)

An intensive 10-week programme combining live masterclasses, mentored workshops, and self-directed modules. Rigorous evaluation of AI models, clinical trial design, and strategic implementation.

- **Format:** online with live sessions and mentoring
- **Duration:** 10 weeks
- **Cost:** 2,990 €
- **Audience:** clinical managers, programme managers
- **Key strength:** data-driven practical approach
- **Language:** English

### Introduction to AI in medicine (University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, UIUC)

A certificate programme for clinicians and non-technical staff. 6 modules covering the fundamental concepts of AI applied to medicine.

- **Format:** self-paced e-learning
- **Duration:** 6-7 hours total | 6-month access
- **Cost:** 750 € | CME credits included
- **Audience:** doctors, nurses, medical assistants
- **Key strength:** concise format, recognised continuing education credits
- **Language:** English

### Discovering the fundamentals of AI (AST-I, France)

A short training specifically designed for healthcare professionals. How AI works, organisational challenges, risks, and benefits.

- **Format:** in-person or remote
- **Duration:** 7 hours
- **Cost:** 350 € excl. VAT per participant
- **Audience:** doctors, prevention specialists, healthcare managers
- **Key strength:** interactive approach adapted to the medical context
- **Language:** French
